Helsinki,Finland

Game Studio and HQ

It's a poorly kept secret that Supercell's first office was a snug 35m2 room that had to fit 15 people,
their laptops and a coffee-maker. Eventually the CEO moved his desk (a cardboard box at the time)
out into the hallway to free up some space. In 2021, after outgrowing a number of locations around
Helsinki, we opened our own, custom-designed office on the seafront, just south of the city center.

The Helsinki office is home to over 250 passionate professionals from various backgrounds and
walks of life. Arounds two thirds of us are directly involved with making games. In addition to five
live teams of roughly 10-25 people each, we have numerous new game teams at prototype stage
working in pairs, trios and more. We mostly stick to speaking in English for work, but you'll hear
chatter in languages from all over the world around the office.

Every week starts with a company breakfast while on Fridays we get together for an all-hands
gathering to share game updates and company news. This often gives way to refreshments, play
sessions and casual conversations.
